Project Brief

Credet is a fintech platform that connects small businesses and individuals with a network of lenders, making it easier for them to access capital. With Credet, financial constraints cannot hold you back from growing your business and solving personal financial problems as we enable you take control of your financial solutions. Credet is simply the loan app that's redefining the borrowing experience of it users.

Project Goals

The research goal is to understand what small business owners in Nigerian feel when they have difficulty accessing capital for their business and what can be done to help them raise capital or business support

Research Method

I used a semi-structured interview to gather in-depth qualitative data. An interview script was used as a guide to ask follow-up questions based on the answers of the users.

Research Objectives

The research objective is to understand what small businesses in Nigerian feel when they have difficulty accessing finance to support their business.

Problem Statement

Small-scale businesses face significant challenges due to the lack of access to adequate capital, hindering their growth and sustainability. This problem statement aims to address the pressing issue of insufficient funding options for these enterprises, leading to restricted expansion opportunities, limited job creation, and reduced economic development in local communities.

Provided Solution

I came up with a solution to create a Fintech platform that connects small Borrowers with a network of lenders and investors, making it easier for them to access capital. the mobile application will be able to:

  • Provide a reliable source of capital to cover operational expenses, invest in marketing, expand her product line, and seize growth opportunities.
  • Intergrate tools and services that can help users streamline administrative tasks and free up more time to focus on business development.
  • Provide assistance with financial planning and forecasting to better manage cash flow and make informed decisions for small scale businesses.
  • Make loans and business financing very flexible for repayment and can be tailored to various user needs.
  • Reduce interest for users to lessen burden of loan repayment.
